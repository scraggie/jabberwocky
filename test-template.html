Alumni Networking Reception and Dinner with President Roderick J. McDavis, Ohio University Foundation Board University staff and fellow alumni
Cleveland Marriott Downtown at Key Center
127 Public Square
Cleveland, OH 44114

July 18 • 6:30-8:30 p.m.

The Ohio University Foundation Board would like to invite Cleveland-area alumni to network with Ohio University President Roderick J. McDavis, Foundation Board Members, University staff and fellow alumni.

The early-bird cost is $20 per person or $35 per couple and includes two complimentary beverages (beer and wine) and heavy hors d'oeuvres.
Parking is NOT included in the price.

Please RSVP online by July 5. For more information, please contact Dawn Werry at 740-593-4300 or werryd@ohio.edu.
